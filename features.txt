# ZERO-WASTE V2 — CIRCULAR ECONOMY PLATFORM FEATURES
A comprehensive breakdown of all panels, screens, and features in the Zero-Waste platform.

---

## 1. DONOR PANEL (General Users & Businesses)
The primary entry point for users wanting to save food, donate excess, and track their impact.

**Home Dashboard (HomeUser.tsx)**
- **Impact Hero Card:** Displays "Meals Rescued", "CO2 Saved", and "Money Saved" using a premium gradient design.
- **Quick Actions:**
  - Save Food (Navigates to Local Savings Market)
  - Donate Bulk (Navigates to Donate Food flow)
  - Ask Food (Navigates to Recipient Food Request)
  - Volunteer (Navigates to Volunteer Rescue Map)
- **Verified Impact Stories:** Horizontal scrolling carousel of `DonorImpactStoryView` proofs uploaded by NGOs.

**Donation Flow (DonateFood.tsx)**
- **Step 1: Food Details:** Specify type (Cooked, Raw, Packaged, etc.) and use the **Device Camera (LiveCamera)** to capture photos of the food.
- **Step 2: Pickup Info:** Enter manual location/address, specify quantity and expiry time.
- **Step 3: Confirmation:** Review details and submit. Triggers notification to NGOs and Volunteers.

**Donation Tracking (DonationTracking.tsx)**
- Live tracking of the donation lifecycle (Listed -> Assigned -> Picked Up -> Arrived -> Verified).
- Live volunteer vehicle map simulation tracking the pickup process.

---

## 2. SHOPKEEPER PANEL (Local Savings Market)
Designed for local bakeries, supermarkets, and restaurants to list near-expiry food at heavy discounts to avoid waste.

**Shopkeeper Setup (ShopkeeperSetup.tsx)**
- 3-Step Wizard: Basic Info, Location & Contact, and Verification Document Upload.

**Dashboard (ShopkeeperDashboard.tsx)**
- **Hero Stats:** Revenue generated, items saved, average discount.
- **Active Listings:** Manage currently listed products.
- **Add Product Wizard:** 3-step flow to upload product photo, set original/discounted price, and expiry countdown.

**Consumer View (LocalSavingsMarket.tsx)**
- Marketplace listing discounted items with urgency badges (e.g., "Expires in 2 hrs").
- Clean bottom-sheet reservation interface (`ProductDetailReserve.tsx`).
- Reservation generates a QR code/reservation ID (`ReservationConfirmation.tsx`).

---

## 3. NGO PANEL (Food Distribution & Verification)
Designed to coordinate large-scale rescues, manage volunteers, and upload proof of impact.

**NGO Login & Setup (NgoLogin.tsx / NgoSetup.tsx)**
- Login for existing verified NGOs.
- Verification flow for new NGOs to upload credentials.

**NGO Feed (NgoFeed.tsx)**
- **Hero Dashboard:** NGO verification badge, total rescues, success rate.
- **Tab 1: Awaiting Action:** View live food donations nearby. Includes an Active Volunteer Directory with click-to-call functionality.
- **Tab 2: Active Pickups:** Track volunteers currently picking up food.
- **Tab 3: Pending Verification:** Food that has arrived at the NGO and needs distribution proof.
- **Tab 4: Completed Stories:** Gallery of completed rescues and generated impact stories.

**Distribution Verification (NgoDistributionProofUploader.tsx)**
- **Step 1: Photo Evidence:** Uses **Device Camera** to capture the actual food distribution to beneficiaries.
- **Step 2: Metrics:** Input the number of people fed.
- **Step 3: Completion:** Generates an official Impact Story and sends it directly to the Donor's dashboard.

**NGO Delivery Tracking (NgoDeliveryTracking.tsx)**
- Tracks the volunteer arriving at the NGO location. Transitions into the Proof Uploader when food arrives.

---

## 4. VOLUNTEER PANEL (Logistics & Rescue)
Designed for delivery agents and community members who transport food from Donors to NGOs.

**Rescue Map (VolunteerMap.tsx)**
- Live map showing pins for available donations (Donors) and destination hubs (NGOs).
- Estimated distances and routing.
- Swiggy-style bottom sheet to accept pickups.

**Volunteer Dashboard (VolunteerHome.tsx)**
- Tracks volunteer impact: Kilometers driven, rescues completed, ranking among other volunteers.
- Active task manager.

---

## 5. RECIPIENT PANEL (Community Support)
Designed for individuals or smaller communities requesting support.

**Recipient Login & Setup (RecipientLogin.tsx / RecipientSetup.tsx)**
- Allows users to log in or set up a profile as an individual or family.

**Home Dashboard (RecipientHome.tsx)**
- Clean interface offering specific community support options.
- Links to Buy Groceries (Marketplace) or Request Food.

**Food Request Form (FoodRequest.tsx)**
- **Support Type:** Choose between Free Food (from donors) or Subsidized Food (from shops).
- **Quantity:** Choose Individual (1-5 people) or Bulk (for events/small NGOs).
- **Multi-Select Food Types:** Select desired items (Cooked Meals, Raw Groceries, Packaged, etc.) with checkboxes.
- **Urgency Level:** Select High, Medium, or Low urgency.
- Submit request to trigger notifications to Donors, NGOs, and Shopkeepers.

---

## PLATFORM-WIDE DESIGN SYSTEM
- **Typography:** Uses Google Fonts (`Outfit` for bold headers, `Plus Jakarta Sans` for clean body text).
- **Aesthetics:** Apple-inspired premium feel. Liquid glass-morphism used in Bottom Navigation bar and overlays.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary Green: `#1A6B3C`
  - Background: Off-white `#F7F5F0`
  - Accents: Amber `#F59E0B` (for warnings/shopkeepers), Red `#DC2626` (urgency), Blue `#2563EB` (requests).
- **Animation:** Framer-motion driven page transitions, tap animations, and floating UI elements.
